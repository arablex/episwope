Seven risk domains

One number. Everything behind it.

Vigilo combines seven risk categories into a single composite score for any country — and shows you every source that moved it.

Disease outbreaks
WHO, CDC, ECDC, ProMED, Africa CDC — confirmed and suspected events
Natural disasters
GDACS, USGS, NASA EONET — earthquakes, floods, volcanoes, wildfires
Conflict & unrest
ACLED, ReliefWeb — armed conflict, protests and civil unrest events
Food safety
FDA, EFSA, WHO recalls — contamination and food-borne illness alerts
Climate events
NOAA, ERA5 baseline — heatwaves, drought, extreme weather patterns
Air quality
OpenAQ, Copernicus — PM2.5, ozone and wildfire smoke indices
Border & transport
IATA travel advisories, border closures, maritime corridor disruptions

How it works

Three steps. Zero jargon.

1

Open the globe

No account. No install. Open vigilo.cc and you're looking at every active risk event, right now, plotted on a live interactive map.

2

Check your destination

Search any country or click any region on the globe to get a risk score from 0–100, broken down by domain — disease, disaster, conflict, food safety, climate, air, borders.

3

Set an alert (optional)

Create a free account to get notified when the risk level in your zone changes. Each alert tells you exactly which event triggered it and which source reported it first.
