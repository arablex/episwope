Honest position

Where Vigilo does not pretend to compete.

We are not a 24/7 operations centre. If a kidnapping happens, Vigilo will not put a helicopter in the air. Crisis24 and International SOS will. If that's what you need, buy that.

We are software, not a services firm. No in-country analysts, no on-the-ground evacuation, no liability indemnity beyond a software contract. We won't sell you a value that requires people we don't have.

We are not SOC 2 today. Targeted for 2027. If your procurement requires SOC 2 already, we're not the right fit yet — say so and we'll explain when we expect to be.

Where we are competitive: transparency, cross-domain breadth, affordability for the mid-market and prosumer segment. That's the whole thesis. If those things matter to you more than 24/7 GOC, we're worth your 15 minutes. If you need an evacuation contract — go to the people who sell that.
