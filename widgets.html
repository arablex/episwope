Embeddable Risk Widgets

Three widgets.
Your brand. One script.

Production-ready embeds showing the live Vigilo Composite Risk Score for any country — fully themeable to a partner brandbook (colours, radius, light/dark). Tune them below; the code updates live.

1 · Location Smart-Card

For hotel / city / destination pages. Composite score, severity meter, top risk domains, AI lead-time, action button.

2 · Checkout Risk Upsell

For the payment cart. A checkbox add-on that sells the PDF safety report. Emits a vigilo:checkout DOM event your cart listens to.

Listen: el.addEventListener('vigilo:checkout', e => e.detail.selected)

3 · Pre-trip Email Alert

Bulletproof table/inline-CSS HTML for newsletters (Outlook/Gmail-safe, no JS). Fill the {{TOKENS}} server-side; colours & radius follow the same theme.
