Honest questions

Things B2B buyers actually ask. Direct answers.

Why invoice-based and not card checkout?
Self-serve card checkout is being added once payment infrastructure is finalised. Until then we send an invoice; you pay by bank transfer, Wise, or crypto (if your accounts payable allows). No surprise auto-charges. Most B2B procurement prefers invoices anyway.
What's the trial?
14 days of the plan you're considering, free. No card required. At the end you decide to subscribe (invoice) or keep using the free tier with reduced limits — we don't auto-charge or block your account abruptly.
How do I cancel?
Reply to your invoice email or write to alex@vigilo.cc. We confirm within 24h and prorate the current period if you cancel in the first 30 days.
What counts as a seat?
One named user with their own login. Generic shared logins are against the terms — we'd rather you use a Team plan honestly than degrade your security.
Do you have a DPA / Security review documents?
Yes — see /trust and /security. Standard GDPR Art. 28 DPA available on request within 48 hours.
What if I'm in a country where Vigilo isn't covered well?
We're transparent about coverage — see the methodology. If your primary country is under-covered, write us before signing; we'd rather under-promise than over-bill.
Can we get a discount?
Annual billing: 2 months free (16% off). Multi-year: discuss. NGOs above the free-tier ceiling but under $20M budget: 30% off Team/Org on application.
