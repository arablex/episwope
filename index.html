The problem

The news scares you. It doesn't inform you.

Health threats are real — but scattered across hundreds of agencies, in dozens of languages, buried in panic headlines.

Fragmented

WHO, CDC, ECDC, national ministries — each in its own silo, format and language.

No context

Is it near you? Trending up or down? How serious — for a traveler vs a resident?

No early signal

By the time it's in your feed, it's old news. No alerts for the places you care about.

Credibility

Official sources. Honest about limits.
