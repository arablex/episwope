## 2. Free-Tier Eligibility (the wall)

> Free is only for orgs we'd otherwise lose to the budget barrier.
> Anyone able to pay pays — or the price anchor dies.

**Free tier — frontline NGO programme:**
- Registered nonprofit / 501(c)(3) / equivalent in country of registration
- Active **field operations** with staff in elevated-risk environments
- Annual operating budget **under $5M** (or under €5M / equivalent)
- Currently NOT subscribed to Crisis24 / International SOS / INSO / Riskline
  / Control Risks (if you are, you have budget — please consider a paid tier)

**Excluded from free tier:**
- Commercial organisations (any size)
- Universities / study-abroad programmes
- Government-funded above $5M annual budget
- Religious / faith organisations purely doing remote work (no field operations)

**In exchange for free access, you grant:**
- Right to use your org name in a short "trusted by" list (you can opt out)
- Reasonable feedback on field experience (one 20-minute call per quarter
  or async equivalent)
- Permission to use anonymised aggregate observations in our research

> Page-ready. Plain, peer-to-peer tone. No marketing-speak.

Free real-time risk monitoring for small humanitarian and development
organisations operating in elevated-risk environments.

**What we ask.**
- Honest field feedback — what was useful, what was noise, what we missed
- Permission to mention you (optional)
- One short call per quarter if you can spare it

**Who qualifies.** Small frontline NGOs, registered nonprofit, field
operations in elevated-risk environments, under $5M annual budget, not
currently subscribed to Crisis24/INSO/IntSOS. [Full criteria.]

**Apply.** Reply to alex@vigilo.cc with: org name, primary country/region

## 4. Operating notes (private — Alex)

- Send from a **non-Resend** inbox (protect the alert-sender domain)
- Cap first cohort at **5–15 design partners** — anything more than that
  you can't onboard properly solo
- Track sent / replies in a simple sheet — month-1 goal: 3–5 active
- When something flags in their region, **email them directly within 24h
  with a short summary** — this is the moment that builds the relationship
- After 30 days of pilot, ask for a quote you can use publicly
