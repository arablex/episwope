Methodology & validation

What we tested. Including what failed.

Every signal is pre-registered, backtested with no lookahead, and only ships if it survives. We publish the full record — the verdicts below are real, committed, and were not tuned.

What we claim — and what we don't

We claim

  • Aggregation of 44 verified data streams across 7 domains
  • A deterministic composite score; every value links to its source line
  • Seasonal & historical (25-yr ERA5) risk baselines
  • Open API, published pricing, published methodology

We do NOT claim

  • Validated outbreak prediction
  • Beating WHO / a lead-time guarantee
  • Black-box ML accuracy
  • Compliance certifications we don't hold

How to read this

Each success criterion was registered before the run. Reconstruction is strictly causal (no lookahead, enforced by unit tests). We never re-subset data or move the bar to manufacture a positive. The validation harness is reusable and lives in the repository. A negative is a valid, committed outcome — that discipline is the product.
